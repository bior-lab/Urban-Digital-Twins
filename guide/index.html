BIOR Urban Digital Twins User Guide

This guide introduces the Urban Digital Twins platform, including its project background, team, data source, and related publications.

About

This BIOR Urban Digital Twin is an interactive web-based platform for urban-scale microclimate-informed building energy analysis.

It integrates building-level energy modelling, urban morphology, measured electricity-use intensity, and WRF-derived microclimate fields to support urban-scale analysis of building energy performance under different climate conditions.

Taking Singapore as a case study, the platform visualizes spatial variations in microclimate exposure and building energy demand at both building and 500 m district scales.

It provides an interactive framework for exploring how relatively hot, relatively cool, and transitional climate conditions influence urban energy use and supports future applications in microclimate-informed urban planning and building energy assessment.

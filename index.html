Urban Digital Twins:
A Tool for Urban-Scale Microclimate-Informed Building Energy Analysis

Project summary

This digital twin integrates building-level energy modeling, urban morphology, measured electricity-use intensity, and WRF-derived microclimate fields to visualize how Singapore's built environment responds to relatively hot, relatively cold, and transitional climate conditions at both building and 500 m district scales.
